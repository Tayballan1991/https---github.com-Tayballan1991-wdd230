Website Planning Document

Target Audience Description

  • Who: People interested in visiting and learing about Roy, Hooper, and Farr West located in Utah. As well as those who live here that want to get out and explore where they live.
  • Age: 10 and up.
  • Technology: The majority of the audience may access the sight through mobile devices, such as cell phones, and ipads. Others will probably use laptops, or home computers.
  • Income: Levels of income may vary according to age and Levels of education (may range from $50,000 and above annually)
  • Motivation: Wanting to know the weather, and traffic forcast to better know who to dress for being outdoors and planning activities. Aslo will know the best route to take to get where they are going.

Personas

James Black

  • Occupation: Banker for Zions Bank
  • Demographic: 25 year old male
  • Goals and Motivation for using the site: He is visiting the area with friend sand is looking for things to do around the area, and how the weather forcast is to outdoor activites.
  • Technology: Hames is never without his phone and uses it to look up actitives, weather, traffic, and news.

Beth Ann Johnson

  • Occupation: Stay at home Mum
  • Demographic: She 35 year old lives in a home with family. Spends most of her time taking care of her young son, taking him to the park on nice days.
  • Goals and Motivation for using the site: Being able to know how to dress her kids to go out, as well to know if there are any activites in the park for kids.
  • Technology: She uses her cell phone to look up most things.

Scenarios
